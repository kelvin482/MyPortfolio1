How I Got Here

Curiosity got me started. I wanted to understand how websites worked, so I learned HTML. Then CSS. Then JavaScript. One thing led to another, and now I'm building full applications.

The moment that hooked me? Watching someone use something I built. That feeling never gets old.

My Approach

My approach is straightforward: understand the problem, build a solution, test it, then improve it. I prefer code that's readable over clever, and designs that work over flashy.

I start by asking questions, sketching ideas, then building. I iterate based on what actually works, not what looks good in theory. Performance and accessibility are not afterthoughts; they are built in from the start.
